📋 Markboard

The native Mac Kanban board for the Markdown task files you already have.

Your tasks live in plain Markdown files on your Mac - readable by you, your editor, and your AI assistant. Markboard is the board on top: drag cards, complete tasks, capture from anywhere. Every change is written straight back into your files. No database, no cloud, no account - your tasks are "### heading" blocks in the files you already own, and if your format is different, Markboard says so honestly instead of importing anything.

Support

Questions, bugs, or ideas: please open an issue on the Markboard issue tracker - it's read regularly, and issues help other users too.

Privacy

Markboard collects nothing. See the privacy policy (spoiler: it's three sentences, because there is no data).
