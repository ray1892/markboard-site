Markboard Privacy Policy

Markboard collects no data. The app has no network access, no analytics, no telemetry, and no accounts. Nothing you do in Markboard ever leaves your Mac.

Your task files are plain Markdown documents in a folder you choose. They are read and written locally and are never transmitted anywhere.

Purchases (Markboard Pro) are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Markboard itself receives no personal information from a purchase - only Apple's anonymous confirmation that the unlock exists.

Effective 2026-07-07. If this policy ever changes, the change will be visible on this page before it applies.
